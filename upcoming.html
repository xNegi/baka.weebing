Chainsaw Man – The Movie: Reze Arc

Release date : 19 September 2025   Rating : 4.1

Chainsaw Man – The Movie: Reze Arc (劇場版『チェンソーマン レゼ篇』) is an anime film adaptation of the Bomb Girl arc of the Chainsaw Man manga by Tatsuki Fujimoto. It is animated by Studio MAPPA and will premiere on September 19, 2025 in Japan. The movie will also release in the United States by Sony Pictures Releasing on October 29, 2025.
