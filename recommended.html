My Recommendation

Haikyu

Release date : Apr 2014   Rating : 4.9

Genre: Sports, Drama, Comedy, School, Shounen

Haikyu!! follows Shoyo Hinata, a passionate short-statured volleyball player, as he joins Karasuno High and teams up with rival Kageyama. Through intense matches and teamwork, the series captures the spirit of sportsmanship, growth, and determination to rise in Japan’s competitive high school volleyball scene.

The Eminence in Shadow

Release date : Oct 2022   Rating : 5.0

Genre: Action, Comedy, Fantasy, Isekai, Magic, Chuunibyou, Dark Fantasy

The Eminence in Shadow centers on Cid Kagenou, a boy obsessed with being a secret mastermind. After reincarnating in a magical world, he unknowingly builds a real shadow organization while thinking it’s just roleplay. The series blends comedy, action, and delusion into a unique take on the isekai genre.

Blue Lock

Release date : Oct 2022   Rating : 4.8

Genre: Sports, Psychological, Thriller, Drama, Shounen

In Blue Lock, Japan launches a controversial project to find the ultimate egoist striker for the national football team. Yoichi Isagi joins the intense survival-style program, where only one forward will remain. The anime dives deep into competitive spirit, ego, ambition, and the psychological battles of aspiring football stars.

Sakamoto Days

Release date : Jan 2025   Rating : 4.4

Genre: Action, Comedy, Slice of Life, Supernatural, Crime

Sakamoto Days follows a legendary hitman, Taro Sakamoto, who retires for a peaceful life with his family and gains weight. But threats from his past pull him back into action. Balancing comedy, explosive action, and clever plot twists, the series showcases a wholesome badass who protects his loved ones.

Spy x Family

Release date : Apr 2022   Rating : 4.6

Genre: Action, Comedy, Slice of Life, Spy, Family, Shounen

In Spy x Family, master spy Twilight must build a fake family to complete a mission. He unknowingly marries an assassin and adopts a telepathic child. As they live undercover, genuine bonds form. This action-comedy blends espionage, heartwarming moments, and chaos in a mission-driven yet wholesome family dynamic.
